How Each Factor Contributes to PCOS

Age & Weight

Higher weight and age can increase insulin resistance and hormonal imbalances, leading to PCOS.

Hormonal Imbalance

Disruptions in hormone levels can cause irregular periods and ovulation issues, key indicators of PCOS.

Hyperandrogenism

Increased male hormone levels lead to acne, excessive hair growth (hirsutism), and other PCOS symptoms.

Insulin Resistance

Leads to weight gain and worsens PCOS by affecting ovulation and increasing androgen production.

Exercise & Sleep

Regular exercise and adequate sleep improve metabolism, hormone regulation, and reduce PCOS risk.

Conception Difficulty

PCOS affects fertility by causing irregular ovulation and affecting the quality of eggs produced.
